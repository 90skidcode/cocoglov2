About This Workshop

Dive into a fun, beginner-friendly workshop where you'll get hands-on experience working with natural ingredients and learn the fundamentals of soap making. Perfect for anyone looking to start their soap-making journey — no prior experience needed!

You will learn the basics, understand the process, and create your own handmade soaps using your choice of colours and scents. Your soaps will be ready to take home after the session.

Workshop Details

This is a 1-day hands-on session designed to introduce you to the art of soap making. You'll learn, create, and leave with your very own handmade soaps — all in a single session.

What You'll Learn

  • Basics of soap making
  • Understanding ingredients & their roles
  • Working with natural materials
  • Understanding oils, fragrances & additives
  • A simple beginner recipe
  • Safe handling of materials
  • Basic design techniques
  • Moulding and finishing your soaps

🎨 Design Process

Explore your creativity by choosing your favourite colours and scents. Create simple yet beautiful soaps using the techniques learned during the session.
