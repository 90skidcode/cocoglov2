About This Workshop

Take your soap-making skills to the next level. This immersive, hands-on workshop is designed for those who want to go beyond the basics and deepen their understanding of cold process soap making. Work with natural ingredients, explore advanced techniques, and create beautifully designed soaps from scratch.

Workshop Schedule

Day 1
Science & Creation

Learn the fundamentals of cold process soap making — how oils and lye come together through saponification. Gain an understanding of recipe formulation and lye calculations, then create your own soap using a 6-slot mould.

Day 2
Replacement & Refinement

Explore replacement techniques — how to substitute oils, liquids, colours, and fragrances while maintaining the quality and balance of your soap.

What You'll Learn

  • Saponification — how oils & lye form soap
  • Calculating correct lye amounts accurately
  • Choosing & balancing oils for skin benefits
  • Superfatting, water discounting & trace control
  • Use of specialty additives (clays, charcoal, milk)
  • Testing pH, hardness, lather & longevity
  • Curing, storage & shelf-life optimisation
  • Creating & customising your own soap batch

Advanced Topics Covered

  • In-depth understanding of saponification and soap chemistry
  • Advanced recipe formulation and precise lye (NaOH/KOH) calculations
  • Customising oil blends for specific skin benefits (cleansing, conditioning, hardness)
  • Superfatting, water discounting, and controlling trace
  • Use of specialty additives — clays, charcoal, milk
  • Testing soap quality: pH, hardness, lather, and longevity
  • Curing, storage, and shelf-life optimisation
  • Cutting, curing, and storing finished soaps
