— The COCOGLO Story

From kitchen to community.

It all started in a small kitchen with a passion for natural beauty and a frustration with chemical-laden commercial soaps. The founder began experimenting with coconut oil, shea butter, and botanicals — and the results were extraordinary.

Word spread quickly. Friends asked for bars. Family demanded more. What started as a personal project quickly grew into COCOGLO — a brand rooted in the belief that what you put on your skin matters as much as what you put in your body.

Today, COCOGLO handcrafts every bar with the same care and intention as that very first batch, and shares the craft through workshops that inspire others to connect with the slow, meditative art of soap-making.
