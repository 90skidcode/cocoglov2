About This Workshop

Get hands-on experience working with natural ingredients and learn how to craft your own soaps at home — from start to finish. Make your own 1 kg soap slab and turn it into 10 beautiful bars. No prior experience needed!

In this immersive 2-day experience you'll learn the A–Z of cold process soap making, the science behind saponification, and create your very own custom soap design — choosing your preferred colours, textures, and scents. Sky is the limit!

Workshop Schedule

Day 1
Saturday, 9th May
⏰ 2:00 pm – 6:00 pm

Learn the science behind cold process soap making and explore design techniques. Create your own soap slab with your chosen colours and scents.

Day 2
Sunday, 10th May
⏰ 4:00 pm – 5:00 pm

Return to cut your soap slab into 10 large bars and take your handmade creations home.

📍 Venue: Cocoglo Studio, Komarapalayam, Namakkal Dt – 638183

After booking, our team will contact you within 24–48 hours with complete details, guide you through the design process, and share the Google Maps location.

What You'll Learn

  • The A–Z of cold process soap making
  • The science behind saponification
  • A basic soap recipe & creating your own formulations
  • How to use a lye calculator and key calculations
  • Safe handling of lye and essential oils
  • Soap-making basics and working with natural materials
  • Cutting and finishing techniques
  • Techniques to create unique soap designs

Techniques Covered

You'll explore a rich variety of artisan techniques during the workshop:

Drop Swirl In-the-Pot Swirl Terrazzo Straight Layers Slant Layers Textured Layers Charcoal Line Golden Mica Line

Design Process

Create your own soap inspired by your favourite things & colours using all the exciting techniques you learn. Create your mood board and make a soap inspired by it using your favourite colours, textures, scents & shapes.

Our team will get in touch with you within 24–48 hours after your payment to guide you towards starting to make your mood board. ✨
